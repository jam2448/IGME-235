My Favorite Dishes

This is a list of of my favorite dishes that I have cooked in the past year. All of the photos taken were cooked by me and taken by me. All recipes used are accessible by clicking the title of each dish if one exists. FYI: all of the meals cooked were absolutely scrumptious!


Barbecue Plate

Barbecue chicken and ribs coated in a homemade barbecue sauce. Served with homemade mac & cheese and Mexican street corn.

CheeseBurger

Double patty smashburger with cheddar cheese, caramelized onions, bacon, and lettuce all on a brioche bun.


Zuppa Toscana

The Olive Garden soup...but better! Soup that contains spicy italian sausage, bacon, potatoes, kale, sauteed onions and garlic, topped with parmesan cheese and red pepper flakes.

Steak and Rice

Medium-rare steak marinated in Brazilian steakhouse seasoning. Served with oven-roasted brussel sprouts, cilantro-lime rice, and mexican street corn.


Mac & Cheese

Cellentani mac & cheese with a four cheese blend: sharp cheddar, pepper jack, mozzarella, and smoked gouda. contains bacon, onions, garlic and topped with panko bread crumbs.

Honey Lemon Chicken

Seasoned chicken coated in a honey lemon reduction. Served in toasted white rice also covered in sauce.

Buffalo Chicken Quesadilla

Seasoned chicken coated in buffalo sauce mixed together with a cheddar and pepper jack blend. Wrapped in a warm tortilla.

Salted Caramel Cheesecake

Not much to it... it's a cheesecake! topped with homemade salted caramel sauce with toffee bits inside the batter.
